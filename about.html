About Journey Within Yoga

Welcome to Journey Within Yoga, where the focus is on stretching not just the body, but the limits of your soul. My name is Harish Suthar, and my yoga journey began at the age of 13 in a small village in Punjab. What started as a school activity has now become a lifelong passion, helping me overcome personal challenges and inspiring me to guide others on their own yoga journey.

By the age of 16, I had already participated in multiple yoga competitions, winning 37 certificates, 7 medals, and 8 trophies at state, district, and national levels. I was also honored with a bronze medal at a national yoga competition held in Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh.

After a brief break due to health issues during the COVID-19 lockdown, I realized how essential yoga was not only for physical fitness but for holistic well-being. This realization led me to resume my practice and inspired me to create this platform, Journey Within Yoga, to share the transformative power of yoga with others.

Why "Journey Within"?

The name Journey Within Yoga reflects my belief that yoga is not just about physical postures, but about looking inward and understanding ourselves better. It's a path to connect with your inner self, cultivate mindfulness, and discover peace in both your body and mind.

Mission

My mission is to make yoga accessible and approachable for everyone, regardless of age or experience level. Whether you're seeking flexibility, strength, stress relief, or spiritual growth, I hope to help you find balance through the art of yoga.

Join the Journey

Join me on this journey of self-discovery, growth, and inner peace. Let's stretch, breathe, and journey within together.
