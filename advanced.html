Advanced Yoga Classes

Master Your Yoga Practice

Our Advanced Yoga Classes are designed for experienced practitioners looking to push their boundaries. These sessions focus on challenging asanas, advanced pranayama (breathing techniques), and in-depth meditation practices.

Scorpion Pose (Vrschikasana)

This pose challenges your balance and flexibility while strengthening your core, arms, and shoulders.

Peacock Pose (Mayurasana)

A balance-focused arm pose that requires wrist, arm, and core strength, with an intense body engagement.

King Pigeon Pose (Kapotasana)

One of the deepest backbends, this pose opens the chest and improves spine flexibility.

Firefly Pose (Tittibhasana)

Firefly pose builds arm strength and balance while stretching the hamstrings.
