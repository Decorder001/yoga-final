Welcome to my Yoga journey

Welcome to "Journey Within Yoga"! My name is Harish, a 19-year-old yoga enthusiast from a small village in Punjab. My journey began at the age of 13, where I discovered the transformative power of yoga. For three incredible years, I immersed myself in practice, earning certificates, medals, and even a bronze medal at a national competition in Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh.
However, life’s challenges led me to step away from yoga for a time, particularly during the pandemic, when I faced health issues that reminded me of yoga’s vital role in my well-being. This experience reignited my passion and commitment to the practice. Today, I’m thrilled to share my journey and inspire others to discover the strength, peace, and joy that yoga can bring into their lives. Join me as we embark on this beautiful path together!
